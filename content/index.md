# A Pedagogical Pathway for Democratic Redistricting

Welcome to this interactive learning resource on democratic redistricting and electoral boundaries in Ireland.

## What You'll Learn

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to understanding how electoral districts are created, why they matter, and how mathematical and computational methods can help ensure fair representation.

## Course Structure

The course is organized into progressive modules:

### Getting Started
- Understand why redistricting matters for democracy
- Find your own Electoral District (ED)
- Learn about personal boundaries and civic engagement

### Module 1 - Understanding the Basics
- The Irish electoral system explained
- How to read and interpret electoral maps
- What makes representation fair

### Interactive Tools
Explore hands-on tools to:
- Find your Electoral District
- Compare different mapping approaches
- Explore parameters that affect redistricting
- Build and test your own scenarios

## How to Use This Resource

This is an interactive learning platform. You can:

1. **Read sequentially** - Follow the modules in order for a structured learning path
2. **Jump to topics** - Use the navigation to explore specific areas of interest
3. **Experiment with tools** - Try the interactive visualizations and tools
4. **Test your knowledge** - Complete exercises and explore real-world data

## Why This Matters

Electoral boundaries shape political representation. Understanding how these boundaries are drawn—and the mathematical and ethical principles involved—is essential for informed democratic participation.

Let's begin your journey into understanding democratic redistricting!
